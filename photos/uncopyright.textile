h1. Uncopyright    

This site and all content and photos contained within are uncopyrighted. I release all claims on copyright and put all the content (photos, articles, logos, etc.) of this website into the public domain.

No permission is needed to copy, distribute, or modify the content of this site. Credit is very much appreciated but not required.

The "source code":src for this website is available online to everyone.
